What is MoneyParce?
MoneyParce is a smart financial management tool designed to help individuals understand and improve their money habits.

Key Features:
- Track income and expenses easily
- Set monthly budgets and receive alerts when you overspend
- Categorize transactions automatically
- Visualize your financial trends clearly
- Access from any device, anytime
