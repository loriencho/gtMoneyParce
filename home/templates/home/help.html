Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about how Money Parce helps you manage your finances easily.

How do I set up my monthly budget?

Go to the dashboard tab and simply enter your desired budget in USD and click "change budget". You can update it anytime based on your financial goals.

Can I track multiple sources of income?

Yes! You can add different income sources, such as salary, freelancing, and investments in the transaction tab. The app will automatically update your total income.

How are transactions categorized?

Transactions are automatically categorized based on merchant details and payment description. You can also manually reassign them if needed.

Can I see reports of my spending?

Absolutely! Our app provides detailed graph, showing you where your money goes each month.

I'm over budget, what can I do?

Try cutting down on hidden expenses like subscriptions and food. Developing good spending habits like cooking for yourself can massively cut down on your monthly bills.

I'm under budget, what should I do?

Congratulations! Consider investing your extra income into savings for later, the only thing you can see is the present so always doubt the future!
